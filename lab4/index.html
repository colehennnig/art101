How to make focaccia bread:
1. Mix flour, salt, yeast
 a) 4 cups flour, 2 tsp yeast
 2. Mix dry ingredients with 2 cups of lukewarm water
 3. Drizzle generous amount of olive oil over mixed dough
 4. Cover bowl with towel and place in fridge for 12-18 hrs
 5. Place dough in baking dish, cover with generous amount of olive oil, let
 sit for 2-4 hrs
 6. Press fingers into dough till fingerprints cover entire area of dough
 7. Sprinkle loads of salt on top of dough
 8. Bake at 425 degrees for 25 minutes
